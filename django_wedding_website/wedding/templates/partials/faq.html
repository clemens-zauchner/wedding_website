How to get to Innsbruck

A flight to Innsbruck directly is very beautiful as you will see a lot of mountains but it can be quite expensive. There is also the option to fly to Munich and to take the train or depending on when you will arrive, an airport taxi. We can recommend fourseasons. Be careful not to book a flight to Munich West/Memmingen. There are no trains there.

How to get to the church?

There is a public bus directly from the city center of Innsbruck to the church. It's line J. With the car it's a 15 min drive. There is free car parking at Congresspark.
